FAQs

Is GeoStamp Camera free to use?

Yes. The app is free to download and offers additional premium features.

Can I add GPS location to my photos?

Yes. Add GPS coordinates, address, and map information directly to your images.

Can I add date and time stamps?

Yes. Customize the date and time format to match your needs.

Does it support weather and compass?

Yes. Supported devices can display weather and compass information on photos.

Can I add my own logo?

Yes. Custom logo support is available for branded photos.

Does it work offline?

Most camera and stamping functions work offline. Some location and map services require internet.

Who should use GeoStamp Camera?

Perfect for construction, engineering, surveying, inspections, field work, real estate, travel and documentation.

Does it support Google Photos?

Yes. The app supports Google Photos sync for compatible workflows.
