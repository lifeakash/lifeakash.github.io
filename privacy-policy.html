Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how AKD Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information in the GeoStamp Camera mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide the core features of the App (stamping location and map data on photos), we require access to certain information:

  • Location Information: We collect and process your real-time geographic location using GPS and network data. This is necessary to determine the address, coordinates, weather, and map preview displayed on your photos.
  • Camera and Media: We require access to your device's camera to take photos and your storage/media library to save the stamped images.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal information such as your device model and system version to ensure compatibility (e.g., for the "Captured by" field).

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are the links to the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Google Play Services (Location and Maps)
  • AdMob (Advertisements)
  • OpenStreetMap (Map Imagery)
  • Yandex (Satellite Imagery)

3. Advertisement (AdMob)

We show advertisements through AdMob during the satellite searching phase. These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to send advertisements directly to your App. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. They may also use other technologies (such as cookies or identifiers) to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and to personalize the advertising content.

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All photos taken with the App are stored directly on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers.
  • No Server Collection: We do not maintain a database of your personal location history or identity on any external servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
